Carepool is a carpooling solution, meant to help you easily find people to carpool with, and to automate the carpooling process.
 
Save gas money, meet new people, and help reduce air pollution!

  • Find people who have similar destinations and schedules
  • Avoid splitting gas by taking turns driving
  • Let Carpool take care of setting up an efficient route for you
  • Find out how much money you are saving
  • Help the environment!
