The Writer's Dilemma

Writing stories is a difficult process.

Novels can be complex and are difficult to organize.

Writers need to constantly review their work to create a consistent product.

Overview

Storytelling is the heart of all communication... People are always telling stories!

We believe you shouldn't have to struggle with technology to write your story!

Easy Revision

Keep your writing consistent by using InkWeaver's easy-to-use platform.

Organize Thoughts

Become more productive by using InkWeaver to organize your writing thoughts. We help writers keep track of important thoughts so that they can write more efficiently. Navigation throughout the web app is intutive and easy to use.

Analyze Writing

InkWeaver takes your writing and gives you easy access to many writing statistics. You will be able to use this data to strengthen your story and better engage your readers. InkWeaver can also help you understand the emotions your readers will feel by utilizing our state-of-the-art Mood-Extraction Algorithmic Techniques (MEAT).
