Overview

Problem

  • Students have a hard time staying on track while studying.
  • Most students have some sort of way to plan their day but it's lacking.
  • This leads to increased anxiety as well as poor grades.
  • Students only use about 65% of their study time for on-task work.
  • Most modern time tracking applications donâ€™t analyze how the user accomplishes their tasks or if they could have been completed more efficiently.

The Solution

Several scheduling applications exist on the market, but are often forgotten about and go unused. They are great at tracking your schedule but fail in one key aspect, they get deleted because the incentive and motivation is just not there. Having a great task tracker application is great but having a way to track tasks and have fun is paramount.

That's where Time Saver comes in! Not only does it track tasks and their due dates, it gives points for completed and graded tasks. User's earn these points and compete against friends to see who can get the most points.
