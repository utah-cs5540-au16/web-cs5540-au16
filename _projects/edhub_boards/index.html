OVERVIEW

Being a student brings to mind images of stressful all-nighters studying for tests, doing homework, and researching dozens upon dozen of sites, which is not including the multiple sites that teachers use for each individual class.

BOARDS is a solution to this problem, allowing student to organize their school content in an intuitive hierarchical manner. BOARDS strives to be easy to use and access, simple, intuitive, and minimal.
