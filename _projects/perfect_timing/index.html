Your Assignment Planning Companion

Perfect Timing helps you plan out your assignments so you can get them done on time, every time! It learns from your personal study habits and those of students who have taken your same classes to determine just how much time you'll need to finish each assignment, and when to work on it. It also works around your existing Google Calendar schedule, so you'll never need to worry about it scheduling over your important events. Never stress out about assignment due dates again!

Features

Plan

Plan your school schedule quickly and accurately. Powerful software that intelligently predicts your schedule for you. Fine tune plans through calendar sync and easy manual adjustment. Never feel weighed down by the scheduling process again.

Compare

Compare your progress with students from your school. Get helpful suggestions for how much time an assignment should take based upon past and current studentsâ€™ experience. See the grading statistics for the current semester as well as past semesters from the archive.

Track

Track your progress and time management improvement. Receive alerts that keep you on time and help you effortlessly record your progress on tasks. When surprises happen, easily adjust your schedule to meet the new circumstances and get back on track.

Design

Documents

The first phase of this project included exploring the problem space of time management. Contextual inquiries were performed to identify critical components for a valuable solution. Several designs were then explored and critiqued to arrive at a refined solution. Documentation for this process can be found in Getting the Right Design. This initial design underwent further review through usability test and heuristic evaluations on a paper prototype. The final paper prototype was then refined into a digital mock up. Documentation detailing the second phase can be found in Getting the Design Right.
