​Overview
Small business owners (SBOs) can have a hard time figuring out what the actual costs of business are, and therefore, what the final price point to the customer should be when accounting for multiple variables. They often do not have a way of keeping track of mileage, material expenses, time, desired profit margin, depreciation etc. in one place. Our solution allows SBOs to have an all in one application that keeps track of all of these things together so they can provide a more accurate and consistent quote to their customers as opposed to haphazardly guessing what they need to charge.
