Overview

Problem and Solution Overview:

When an engineering student is not able to entirely focus on a particular task, he can distract himself for the whole duration he planned to finish it. Maybe it started as a search for some important piece of information he needed, but it quickly turns into 4 hours of browsing Facebook, watching YouTube and playing video games. An engineering student can enjoy too many entertainments using a computer, which they use every day. With poor self-control, one can lose much needed time without the intention to waste it. Students may want something to force them doing the things which they have to do.
